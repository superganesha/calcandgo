About CalcAndGo

Built for anyone who needs a fast, trustworthy answer to an everyday math or financial question.

Our Mission

CalcAndGo exists because doing math shouldn't require a finance degree, a spreadsheet, or sitting on hold with a bank. We built a collection of free, accurate, and easy-to-use calculators that give anyone — students, first-time homebuyers, travelers, healthcare workers, parents — instant access to the numbers they need to make better decisions.

Every tool on CalcAndGo is designed with one principle in mind: give you the answer and explain how we got there. We don't just spit out a number — we show the formula, break down the components, and provide context so you actually understand what the result means.

What We Offer

Our calculator suite covers the situations that come up most often in real life:

  • Mortgage Calculator: Full PITI breakdown with property tax estimates for all 50 states and Washington D.C., plus a complete amortization schedule. Because knowing your monthly payment is just the start — you need to understand where that money is actually going.
  • Loan Calculator: Monthly payment and total interest for any loan type (personal, auto, student), plus an extra payment simulator that shows exactly how much time and money you save by paying a little more each month.
  • BMI & Health Calculator: Body Mass Index with category classification, Basal Metabolic Rate (using the Mifflin-St Jeor formula, the gold standard used by clinicians), and Total Daily Energy Expenditure to help estimate calorie needs for weight management goals.
  • Tip Calculator & Bill Splitter: Instantly calculates tips for any percentage and splits the total evenly among any number of people. Includes a full tipping etiquette guide.
  • Percentage Calculator: Four distinct modes covering every percentage question we've ever heard: finding a percent of a number, identifying what percent one value is of another, computing percentage change, and reverse percentage calculations.
  • Unit Converter: Eight measurement categories — length, weight, volume, temperature, area, speed, time, and digital storage — with dozens of units each. Metric and imperial both fully supported.
  • Advanced Math Solver: Algebra, calculus, matrix operations, number base conversion, and Boolean logic tools powered by the math.js library. Useful for students, engineers, and developers.

Our Commitment to Accuracy

We take calculation accuracy seriously. All financial formulas (mortgage, loan, amortization) use standard actuarial math. Property tax rates reflect the most current available state average effective rates. Health calculations use clinically validated formulas. Unit conversions use exact defined ratios wherever they exist.

That said, results are estimates. Mortgage and loan calculations don't account for every lender's specific terms. Property tax rates vary enormously by county and municipality. Health numbers are population averages, not personalized medical advice. We always recommend confirming important financial or health decisions with a licensed professional.

Privacy and Your Data

CalcAndGo does not collect, store, or transmit your calculation inputs. All calculations happen entirely in your web browser using JavaScript — your numbers never leave your device. We don't require an account, email address, or any personal information. We use standard web analytics to understand how people use the site, but no individual calculation data is ever recorded.

The site is supported by advertising through Google AdSense. Ads are displayed by Google based on general interest categories, not on your specific calculation inputs, which we never have access to. See our Privacy Policy for complete details.

Our Philosophy on Free Tools

Many financial calculators online serve as lead generators — they give you a number, then immediately try to sell you a product or collect your contact information. We don't do that. CalcAndGo is a reference tool, not a sales funnel. We believe access to financial information and math tools shouldn't depend on your willingness to hand over your email address or be called by a sales rep.

We keep the site free through non-intrusive advertising. This lets us maintain and improve the tools over time without any cost to you.
