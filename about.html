About CalcHub

CalcHub is a free collection of everyday calculators built to help with quick, accurate decisions - from mortgage and loan planning to BMI, tipping, percentages, and unit conversions.

Every calculator runs directly in your browser. Nothing you type is sent to a server or stored anywhere.
